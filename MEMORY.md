## Polymarket Betting Strategy: The Oracle Gap

This strategy is designed to identify potential price discrepancies on Polymarket by exploiting the "Oracle Gap." The core thesis is that while global markets (like ETH/USDT perpetual contracts) react to news in milliseconds, prediction pools on Polymarket may have a delay in adjusting their odds.

### Framework:
1.  **Monitor:** Continuously monitor real-time liquidity and price action on primary financial markets.
2.  **Analyze:** Concurrently analyze the odds within related Polymarket prediction pools.
3.  **Execute:** When a significant discrepancy (a gap) is detected, attempt to capture the spread through rapid trade execution on Polymarket.
